Hello, I'm Ahmad Chabayta

I'm a mechanical and design engineer who's always looking for new opportunities to learn from experienced professionals and work alongside them.

Top Expertise

Maker

I enjoy making stuff and have been doing it since I was a kid. As a maker, my projects start from scratch, either with a piece of wood, metal, plastic, or leather. Each project teaches me a lot and stretches the bounds of my knowledge. I make many mistakes but they serve as an excellent teacher. My ultimate plan is to open up a workshop and make things for a living.

Design Engineering

Product Development

The product vision is the backbone of product development and I define it with market and technical research. Ensuring safety and efficiency is vital for creating a successful product. I design to achieve functionality and practicality, but before that, the product specifications have to be determined according to the needs of the consumer. After refining the product I usually use a 3d printer to guarantee its functions accordingly.
